# Cautionary Notes on Disinformation and the Origins of Distrust – MediaWell

**Source:** [mediawell.ssrc.org](https://mediawell.ssrc.org/expert-reflections/cautionary-notes-on-disinformation-benkler/) · **Published:** 2019-10-22 · **Feed tags:** community, addedbuzz, addedcommunity, buzz

> Yochai Benkler evaluates the effectivness of disinformation campaigns and how they have been elevated as a cause of the crisis of democratic society.
> 
> He suggests that "we may need to understand the political confusion less as a function of intentional, discrete propagandist interventions (though it is those too), and more as one facet of the more basic dynamic of the past decade: the collapse of neoliberalism in the Great Recession and the struggle to define what the new settlement that replaces it must become."
> 
> 10/17/2019, Social Science Research Council, MediaWell
