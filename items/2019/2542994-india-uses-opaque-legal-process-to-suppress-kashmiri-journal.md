# India uses opaque legal process to suppress Kashmiri journalism, commentary on Twitter - Committee to Protect Journalists

**Source:** [cpj.org](https://cpj.org/blog/2019/10/india-opaque-legal-process-suppress-kashmir-twitter.php) · **Published:** 2019-10-24 · **Feed tags:** community, addedcommunity, buzz

> The Committee to Protect Journalists used Lumen in an investigation of Indian government censorship of Kashmiri journalistm.
> 
> "In search of answers, CPJ retrieved requests sent by Indian authorities to Twitter between August 2017 and August 2019 from Lumen. Lumen lists requests received from tech companies themselves, though it is not a complete record; Twitter says it sends requests to Lumen unless 'prohibited from doing so.'"
> 
> 10/24/2019, Committe to Protect Journalists
