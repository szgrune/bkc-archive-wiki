# Building a More Honest Internet - Columbia Journalism Review

**Source:** [cjr.org](https://www.cjr.org/special_report/building-honest-internet-public-interest.php) · **Published:** 2019-12-01 · **Feed tags:** community, addedcommunity, buzz

> While the effect is small, Harvard Law professor Jonathan Zittrain observed that even this slight push could influence an election—Facebook could selectively mobilize some voters and not others. Election results could also be influenced by both Facebook and Google if they suppressed information that was damaging to one candidate or disproportionately promoted positive news about another.
