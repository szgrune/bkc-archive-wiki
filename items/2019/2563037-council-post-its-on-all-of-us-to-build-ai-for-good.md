# Council Post: It's On All Of Us To Build AI For Good

**Source:** [forbes.com](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbesbusinesscouncil/2019/11/22/its-on-all-of-us-to-build-ai-for-good/) · **Published:** 2019-11-25 · **Feed tags:** community, skip

> Creative solutions can encourage responsible AI development. For example, the Data Nutrition Project, a collaboration between the Berkman Klein Center at Harvard University and the MIT Media Lab, is testing a prototype for a dataset “nutrition label.” Its list of “ingredients” makes it easier for people training AI algorithms to evaluate the quality of the datasets they intend to use and identify missing data.
