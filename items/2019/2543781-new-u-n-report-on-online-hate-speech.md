# New U.N. Report on Online Hate Speech

**Source:** [lawfareblog.com](https://www.lawfareblog.com/new-un-report-online-hate-speech) · **Published:** 2019-10-25 · **By:** Evelyn Douek · **Feed tags:** community, addedcommunity

> Mark Zuckerberg, Facebook’s CEO, recently gave a highly promoted [speech](https://newsroom.fb.com/news/2019/10/mark-zuckerberg-stands-for-voice-and-free-expression/) about the importance of protecting freedom of expression online—which was immediately [criticized](https://www.nytimes.com/2019/10/24/opinion/facebook-zuckerberg-free-speech.html) as taking too binary a view of the issues and presenting a false choice between free expression and Chinese censorship. By contrast, Kaye’s report is a very real reckoning with the trade-offs involved in protecting free speech while dealing with the real harm caused by some forms of expression, and is an attempt to find guiding, consistent standards. There is still a lot of work to be done, not least by the companies themselves, to make this a reality. But this latest report will be a useful and influential guide in that process.
