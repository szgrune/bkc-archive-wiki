# Designing AI with Justice | Public Books

**Source:** [publicbooks.org](https://www.publicbooks.org/designing-ai-with-justice/) · **Published:** 2019-10-23 · **Feed tags:** community, buzz, addedcommunity

> Sasha Costanza-Chock on how the design of sociotechnical systems can be one means of dismantling or transforming systems of oppression.
> 
> He discusses: design justice as both community of practice and framework for analysis; how people are already reisisting oppressive AI; and the ten principles of design justice. "[D]esign justice goes beyond fairness. It entails thinking about the matrix of domination—about intersecting systems of oppression—and what it means to design sociotechnical systems that can transform or overturn these systems, rather than constantly reproducing them in technology, in design, and in machine learning."
>
