# Healthcare general counsels explore pressing health policy and legal issues at Harvard Law School - Harvard Law Today

**Source:** [today.law.harvard.edu](https://today.law.harvard.edu/health-care-general-counsels-explore-pressing-health-policy-and-legal-issues-at-harvard-law-school/) · **Published:** 2019-12-12 · **Feed tags:** addedcommunity, community

> Harvard Law School’s WilmerHale Clinical Professor of Law Christopher Bavitz, managing director of the school’s Cyberlaw Clinic, led the group in a discussion of the opportunities and challenges that artificial intelligence, machine learning, and algorithms present to the health care industry. The spirited conversation that followed raised questions on how to conceptualize the role of AI in health care decisions. Should AI be used as tool, or decision-maker? Or should it be viewed as a product or service, or even in some sense a “hire” for the system?
