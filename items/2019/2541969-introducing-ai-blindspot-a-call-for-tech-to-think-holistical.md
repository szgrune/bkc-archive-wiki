# Introducing AI Blindspot: A Call for Tech to Think Holistically and Spot Risks

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/berkman-klein-center/ai-blindspot-be458ef0ab89) · **Published:** 2019-10-23 · **Feed tags:** community, buzz, addedcommunity

> The AI Blindspot project "aims to stimulate discussion and deliberation about the pitfalls of AI systems."
> 
> The project was created during the 2019 Assembly program, organized by the Berkman Klein Center and MIT Media Lab. Team members will be at MozFest 2019, "engaging with stakeholders...to conduct user tests, surveys, and workshops to gather feedback and iterate the content, format, and presentation" of the project's tools.
> 
> 10/22/2019, Berkman Klein Center, Medium
