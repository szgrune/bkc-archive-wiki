# Full article: ‘My body, my money’: SpankChain and the intersection between sex work and cryptocurrencies imaginaries

**Source:** [tandfonline.com](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/23268743.2026.2659125) · **Published:** 2026-05-07 · **Feed tags:** community

> Fac Assc Samer Hassan, who writes: "We do a mixed-methods analysis of a digital sex work platform using crypto. The platform framed itself as a response to their financial exclusion and online censorship. We explore its gendered asymmetries and techno-solutionist narratives analyzing 3,831 comments across 214 subreddits, 100+ blog posts, and other docs. In practice, it is yet another example that technologies for marginalized communities need to be designed with them, not merely for them. The highlights of our findings are summarized in the Bluesky thread below.
