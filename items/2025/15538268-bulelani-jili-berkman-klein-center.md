# Bulelani Jili | Berkman Klein Center

**Source:** [cyber.harvard.edu](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/bulelani-jili) · **Published:** 2025-08-28 · **Feed tags:** bkc-happenings

> Faculty Associate Bulelani Jili: "I wanted to share a few upcoming events I’ll be participating in this fall that may be of interest for the bkc news. Each reflects ongoing work related to digital governance, Africa-China relations, and the politics of emerging technologies:
> 
> 	Africa AI Village at UNGA 80 (September 26, 2025 – United Nations, New York) This landmark event, hosted in collaboration with the African Union, UNICEF GenU, the Commonwealth, and Smart Africa, will spotlight Africa’s emerging leadership in AI and digital innovation. The initiative aims to advance ethical, inclusive, and locally grounded AI ecosystems.
> 
> 	The Age of Possibility: Science, Sovereignty, and Shared Futures (October 16, 2025 – Geneva, Switzerland) As part of the Geneva Science and Diplomacy Anticipation Summit, I’ll be engaging in conversations on scientific advancement, digital sovereignty, and international cooperation.
> 
> 	Future Pasts in Digitalization and Datafication Processes (November 19, 2025 – American Anthropological Association Annual Meeting, New Orleans) I’ll be participating in this two-day conference examining questions of agency, dependency, and the “China question” in African digital trade relationships.
