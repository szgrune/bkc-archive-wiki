> With so many proposals for new international AI initiatives, this article addresses an often neglected topic: funding. It reviews the budgets of 20 existing international organizations (IOs) to estimate the cost of a new AI IO based on its function and offers five key principles for designing a successful funding mechanism. Without reliable funding, a new international AI organization will merely fragment the international ecosystem and dilute funding for AI-related initiatives.
