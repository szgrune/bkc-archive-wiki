# Report: Towards Substantive Equality in Artificial Intelligence: Transformative AI Policy for Gender Equality and Diversity (November 2024)

**Source:** [gpai.ai](https://gpai.ai/projects/responsible-ai/towardsrealdiversityandgenderequalityinai/) · **Published:** 2024-11-28 · **Feed tags:** community

> Paola Ricaurte (expert committee member) writes:
> 
> 	Yesterday we launched the GPAI report [Towards Substantive Equality in Artificial Intelligence: Transformative AI Policy for Gender Equality and Diversi](https://gpai.ai/projects/responsible-ai/towardsrealdiversityandgenderequalityinai/towards-substantive-equality%20in-artificial-intelligence_Transformative-AI-policy-for-gender-equality-and-diversity.pdf)ty and the[Policy Guide for Implementing Transformative AI Policy Recommendations](https://gpai.ai/projects/responsible-ai/towardsrealdiversityandgenderequalityinai/policy-guide-for-implementing-transformative-AI-policy-recommendations.pdf). This has been a global and collaborative effort to reframe the conversation about equality and diversity within AI ecosystems. Over the past two years, we have conducted consultations with individuals and communities worldwide to draft the policy recommendations that emerged from these discussions. We hope the report proves useful for your work and activism around equitable and diverse technological development. Some members from this community were involved, including Petra Molnar, Dragana Kaurin & Jess Fjeld."
