"It's rare to see Africa news getting widespread blogosphere attention, 
and rarer to see good news from Africa get major online attention. But 
the top headline from BBC currently amplified is an optimistic story 
about a discovery of water in Darfur and the possibility that this 
massive underground lake could lead towards a reduction of conflict in 
western Sudan."
Excerpted from 's blogpost, "The news we share: water in Darfur"
[http://www.ethanzuckerman.com/blog/2007/07/19/the-news-we-share-water-in-darfur/](http://www.ethanzuckerman.com/blog/2007/07/19/the-news-we-share-water-in-darfur/)
About Ethan Zuckerman:
[http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/home/ethan_zuckerman](http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/home/ethan_zuckerman)

"On May 11th, Oiwan Lam (pictured at left protesting media content 
controls) committed what she says was a deliberate act of civil 
disobedience.
Writing on the citizen media website InMedia Hong Kong, Oiwan called on 
her readers to post links to erotic websites and also included an artsy 
photo of a topless woman that she found on Flickr."
Excerpted from 's blogpost, "Hong Kong, obscenity laws, and Flickr's 
regional censorship"
[http://rconversation.blogs.com/rconversation/2007/07/hong-kong-obsce.html](http://rconversation.blogs.com/rconversation/2007/07/hong-kong-obsce.html)
About Rebecca MacKinnon:
[http://rconversation.blogs.com/about.html](http://rconversation.blogs.com/about.html)

"Brad Spitz reports in his blog that a French court held DailyMotion 
liable for copyright infringement, despite concluding that the site was 
a mere 'hosting service.' DailyMotion is an online video-sharing site 
similar to YouTube. In a July 13 ruling (in French), the court went out 
of its way to label DailyMotion a hosting service, an argument 
DailyMotion itself put forth."
Excerpted from Kyle Junik's blogpost, "French Court Finds DailyMotion 
Liable For Copyright Infringement"
[http://www.citmedialaw.org/french-court-finds-dailymotion-liable-copyright-infringement](http://www.citmedialaw.org/french-court-finds-dailymotion-liable-copyright-infringement)
About Citizen Media Law Project:
[http://www.citmedialaw.org/about](http://www.citmedialaw.org/about)
