# GIF IT UP 2017

**Source:** [woobox.com](http://woobox.com/v3qwr9) · **Published:** 2017-09-21 · **Feed tags:** buzz, orbit

> "To enter GIF IT UP, you need to make a GIF with material found in one of four sources: [Europeana](http://www.europeana.eu/), [DPLA](http://dp.la/), [DigitalNZ](http://www.digitalnz.org/) or [Trove](http://trove.nla.gov.au/), or a combination GIF with material from several of these websites. Your source material must be clearly marked as: in the public domain, have a ‘no known copyright restrictions’ statement or have a Creative Commons license which allows for reuse."
