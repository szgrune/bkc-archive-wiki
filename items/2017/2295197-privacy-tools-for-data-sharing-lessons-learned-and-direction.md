# Privacy Tools for Data Sharing: Lessons Learned and Directions Forward | Harvard University Privacy Tools Project

**Source:** [privacytools.seas.harvard.edu](https://privacytools.seas.harvard.edu/privacy-tools-data-sharing-lessons-learned-and-directions-forward) · **Published:** 2017-10-12 · **Feed tags:** events

> Privacy Tools for Data Sharing: Lessons Learned and Directions Forward Monday, December 11th, 2017 8:30 AM – 5:00 PM EST. Please visit our Eventbrite page to register. Spring 2018 marks the end of an interdisciplinary, 5-year NSF Secure and Trustworthy Cyberspace Frontier Project “Privacy Tools for Sharing Research Data” at Harvard University. This one-day workshop will bring past and present project participants together with potential users of privacy tools from academia, government, and industry to examine the goals and accomplishments of the project (including practical tools for sharing privacy-sensitive research data). The workshop will discuss the lessons learned about both data privacy and interdisciplinary research and will scope out opportunities for future research, applications, and collaborations going forward.
