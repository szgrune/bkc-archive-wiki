# [PUBLIC] Digital Infrastructure Fund Program Manager - Request for Concept Note - Google Docs

**Source:** [docs.google.com](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1VggnXOkhZWETWaHb9kwHf-wgUg4RUe4bfCmVUNQHXXw/edit) · **Published:** 2022-12-14 · **Feed tags:** oa.infrastructure, oa.jobs, oa.new, opportunities

> "The funding and operational partners are relaunching the Digital Infrastructure Fund with a two-year commitment to grantmaking, convening and growth. We are looking for a program manager to work with the Digital Infrastructure Research Program for up to two years beginning in Q1 2023. This is the third round of the program, and summaries & results from the first year can be seen here. For more about the core values of the fund please review this draft principles document.
> 
> This consultant will coordinate the activities of the fund such as grantmaking, convening and fundraising as well as help design the future direction of the fund. The funding partners are committed to supporting this fund over the next two years and this role will be crucial in helping grow and sustain the fund during this period and beyond...."
