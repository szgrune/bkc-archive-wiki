# Global Data Law - Digitales Forschungsmagazin

**Source:** [digital.uni-passau.de](https://www.digital.uni-passau.de/index.php?id=306&L=1) · **Published:** 2022-01-19 · **Feed tags:** goodid

> After our first Global Data Law Conference with inter alia the wonderful Urs Gasser, the University of Passau Research Centre for Law and Digitalisation (FREDI) is extremely honored to co-organize its second conference with the fantastic University of Ghana School of Law. Please see attached our Call for Papers – please share and / or submit! The conference will focus on the developments of [Data Protection Laws in Africa](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3947664) and will take place in Accra, Ghana. It is a three-days conference from 13th - 15th of September 2022 and is intended to bring together a range of experts and practitioners from across the continent and beyond to discuss African data protection laws and data policies – from regional to national perspectives. Further information also [here](https://www.digital.uni-passau.de/index.php?id=306&L=1).
