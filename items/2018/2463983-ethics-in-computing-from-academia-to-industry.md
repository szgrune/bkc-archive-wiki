# Ethics in Computing, from Academia to Industry

**Source:** [infoq.com](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/ethics-academia-industry) · **Published:** 2018-10-04 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> [Kathy Pham](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/kpham) highlights considerations of ethics, social responsibility, and long-term impacts of software industry products. [Pham covers](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/ethics-academia-industry) actions being taken in academia and industry today, as well as actionable paths forward to create cultures that build more responsible software and services for all people.
> 
> She also spoke on a [panel](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/panel-ethics-computing) discussing important points around privacy, security, safety online, and intent of software today.
