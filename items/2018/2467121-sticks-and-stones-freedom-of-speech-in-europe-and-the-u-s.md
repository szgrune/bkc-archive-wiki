# Sticks and Stones: Freedom of Speech in Europe and the U.S.

**Source:** [magazine.areweeurope.com](https://magazine.areweeurope.com/the-ocean-between-us/stories/sticks-and-stones) · **Published:** 2018-11-12 · **Feed tags:** mailchimp, website, buzz

> “The First Amendment is rather unique. Free speech is widely protected with very few exceptions,” explains [Nani Jansen Reventlow](https://clinic.cyber.harvard.edu/bio/nani-jansen-reventlow/), an international lawyer and passionate human rights advocate, who has litigated crucial freedom of expression cases and runs the [Digital Freedom Fund](https://www.digitalfreedomfund.org/), an organization that supports court litigation on privacy rights and free speech. According to her, European countries attach more duties and responsibilities to freedom of speech than the United States does.
