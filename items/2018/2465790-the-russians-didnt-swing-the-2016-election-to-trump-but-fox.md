# The Russians didn’t swing the 2016 election to Trump. But Fox News might have. - The Washington Post

**Source:** [washingtonpost.com](https://www.washingtonpost.com/outlook/2018/10/24/russians-didnt-swing-election-trump-fox-news-might-have/?noredirect=on) · **Published:** 2018-10-25 · **Feed tags:** website, mailchimp, buzz

> The Russians didn’t swing the 2016 election to Trump. But Fox News might have.
> 
> “All the Russians did was jump on the right-wing propaganda bandwagon: Their efforts were small in scope, relative to homegrown media efforts. And what propaganda victories the Russians achieved occurred only when the right-wing media machine picked up stories and, often, embellished them,” writes BKC co-director [Yochai Benkler](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/ybenkler) in this [Washington Post Op-Ed](https://www.washingtonpost.com/outlook/2018/10/24/russians-didnt-swing-election-trump-fox-news-might-have/?noredirect=on&utm_term=.b74b23488e8b) about his new book “Network Propaganda,” written with BKC research director [Rob Faris](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/rfaris) and fellow [Hal Roberts](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/hroberts).
