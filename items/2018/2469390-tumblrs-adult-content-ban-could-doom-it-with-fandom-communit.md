# Tumblr’s adult-content ban could doom it with fandom communities.

**Source:** [slate.com](https://slate.com/technology/2018/12/tumblr-fandom-adult-content-ban-livejournal.html) · **Published:** 2018-12-05 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> What happens when (a) the user base of a social platform includes a large number of fan creators, LGBTQ people, and support communities; and (b) that platform starts banning content it deems inappropriate? After [Tumblr’s recent announcement](https://slate.com/technology/2018/12/tumblr-porn-should-be-welcomed-not-banned.html) that it will no longer allow “adult content” (defined, in part, as “[female-presenting nipples](https://support.tumblr.com/post/180758979032/updates-to-tumblrs-community-guidelines)“) on the platform, many have been asking this exact question. However, we don’t need to peer into the future to answer it—we can just rewind to 10 years in the past.
