# No Longer Running Naked through the Digital World

**Source:** [blogs.harvard.edu](http://blogs.harvard.edu/doc/2018/10/12/naked/) · **Published:** 2018-10-12 · **By:** Doc Searls · **Feed tags:** website, mailchimp, buzz

> [No Longer Running Naked through the Digital World](http://blogs.harvard.edu/doc/2018/10/12/naked/)
> 
> In his most recent [Weblog](http://blogs.harvard.edu/doc/2018/10/12/naked/) and [TEDx Talk](http://tedxsantabarbara.com/2018/doc-searls/), Berkman Klein alumni Doc Searls covers the collateral effects of being human in a rapidly increasing digital age. "We live in two worlds now: the natural one where we have bodies that obey the laws of gravity and space/time, and the virtual one where there is no gravity or distance (though there is time). In other words, we are now digital as well as physical beings, and this is new to a human."
