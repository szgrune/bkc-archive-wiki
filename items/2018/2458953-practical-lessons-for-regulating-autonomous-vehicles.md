# Practical lessons for regulating autonomous vehicles

**Source:** [news.itu.int](https://news.itu.int/autonomous-vehicles-regulation-lessons/) · **Published:** 2018-08-07 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> "Autonomous Vehicles (AVs) are complex," says Berkman Klein Fellow[Aida Joaquin Acosta](http://cyber.harvard.edu/people/ajoaquinacosta) in [this interview](https://news.itu.int/autonomous-vehicles-regulation-lessons/)with ITU News, summing up key points from [4 new policy papers on the subject](http://cyber.harvard.edu/story/2018-07/4-policy-papers-autonomous-vehicles). "AVs may challenge cybersecurity, privacy, ethical norms, environmental and landscaping schemes, mobility and accessibility paradigms, use of resources, or sectorial employment."
