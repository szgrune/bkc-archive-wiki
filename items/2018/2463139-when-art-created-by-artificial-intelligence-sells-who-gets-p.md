> When Art Created by Artificial Intelligence Sells, Who Gets Paid?
> 
> [Jessica Fjeld](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/jfjeld), assistant director of the [Cyberlaw Clinic](http://clinic.cyber.harvard.edu/) at Harvard Law School, lends insight into the discussion of AI-created art for [this article](https://www.artsy.net/article/artsy-editorial-art-created-artificial-intelligence-sells-paid). “For me, the far more interesting question is who among these people acquire rights in the outputs, not whether the software itself could have any claim of ownership,” Fjeld says in the article.
