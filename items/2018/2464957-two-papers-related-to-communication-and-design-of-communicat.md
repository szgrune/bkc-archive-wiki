# Two papers related to communication and design of communication systems

**Source:** [trusttri.github.io](https://trusttri.github.io/papers/final_version_CSCW_2018.pdf) · **Published:** 2018-10-17 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> [Deliberation and Resolution on Wikipedia: A Case Study of Requests for Comments](https://trusttri.github.io/papers/final_version_CSCW_2018.pdf)
> 
> Fellow Amy X Zhang shares two papers related to communication and design of communication systems, one in the context of [Slack discussion groups](http://people.csail.mit.edu/axz/papers/cscw_tilda.pdf) and difficulties keeping up with them, and [another](https://trusttri.github.io/papers/final_version_CSCW_2018.pdf) in the context of deliberations on Wikipedia and difficulties resolving them. The Slack paper received a Best Paper Award.
