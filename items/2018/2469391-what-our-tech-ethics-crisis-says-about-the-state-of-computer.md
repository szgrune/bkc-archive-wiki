# What Our Tech Ethics Crisis Says About the State of Computer Science Education

**Source:** [howwegettonext.com](https://howwegettonext.com/what-our-tech-ethics-crisis-says-about-the-state-of-computer-science-education-a6a5544e1da6) · **Published:** 2018-12-05 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> We see evidence of this frame of mind — that ethics is only a specialization, something that someone else does — every time anyone says “I just build things” when questioned about the ethics of a particular technology... The problem with “I’m just an engineer” isn’t the engineer’s inability to identify all relevant ethical implications — it’s that they don’t think it’s their jobto do so.
