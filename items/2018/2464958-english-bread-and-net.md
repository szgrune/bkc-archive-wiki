# English - BREAD&NET

**Source:** [breadandnet.org](https://www.breadandnet.org/english.html) · **Published:** 2018-10-15 · **Feed tags:** orbit

> From November 19-21, my organization SMEX, along with several other organizations and sponsors, will host the inaugural Bread & Net Gathering in Beirut, Lebanon. Bread&Net is a multi-day unconference and social gathering, where activists, technologists, lawyers, artists, trainers, journalists, researchers, entrepreneurs and others from across the Arabic-speaking world will come together to strengthen collective efforts to advance human rights in digitally networked spaces. The three-day gathering will consider how to create space for policy reform in difficult contexts, opportunities for language and localization, intersections between individuals and communities in the digital sphere, and how new modes of cultural production can help us develop new narratives of resistance. Session proposals are closed but registration (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfHBlikQn8Ev1uOme0XTd5DA4VqdY4o9BLBgkHTFoxXww93cQ/viewform) is still open. The event will comprise sessions in both English and Arabic.
