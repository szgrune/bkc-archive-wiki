# You Are Entering an Ephemeral Bio-allowed Data Capture Zone

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/@judithd/you-are-entering-an-ephemeral-bio-allowed-data-capture-zone-5ecafd2dbdaf) · **Published:** 2018-08-08 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> You Are Entering an Ephemeral Bio-allowed Data Capture Zone
> 
> Faculty fellow [Judith Donath](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/jdonath)argues against a ban on facial recognition in [this Medium](https://medium.com/@judithd/you-are-entering-an-ephemeral-bio-allowed-data-capture-zone-5ecafd2dbdaf) post, suggesting instead that “we need to seek regulation based on the qualities and capabilities of the technology, not the technology itself.” Donath proposes a list of categories that could be used as a starting point for developing a taxonomy for thinking through privacy-enhanced identifying technologies.
