# John Perry Barlow and the Foundational Values of the Net

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/berkman-klein-center/john-perry-barlow-and-the-foundational-values-of-the-net-c27cf5385478) · **Published:** 2018-05-10 · **Feed tags:** buzz

> Internet pioneer John Perry Barlow [passed away](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2018/02/john-perry-barlow-internet-pioneer-1947-2018) last week at the age of 70. As the [Berkman Klein Center’s inaugural fellow in 1998](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/jbarlow), his ideas helped provide a foundation for much of the work of our community in the two decades since. Berkman Klein Center Founder and Director Charles Nesson shared his thoughts on how Barlow helped build the values of the Internet.
