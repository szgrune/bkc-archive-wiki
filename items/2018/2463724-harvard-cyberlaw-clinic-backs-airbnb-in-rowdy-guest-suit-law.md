# Harvard Cyberlaw Clinic Backs Airbnb In Rowdy Guest Suit - Law360

**Source:** [law360.com](https://www.law360.com/commercialcontracts/articles/1087319/harvard-cyberlaw-clinic-backs-airbnb-in-rowdy-guest-suit) · **Published:** 2018-10-01 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Harvard Cyberlaw Clinic Backs Airbnb In Rowdy Guest Suit
> 
> Our [Cyberlaw Clinic](http://clinic.cyber.harvard.edu) has urged the Ninth Circuit court not to touch a lower court decision ending a corporate landlord's lawsuit accusing Airbnb of helping tenants break building rules and host rowdy guests, [saying that a revival of the suit](https://www.law360.com/commercialcontracts/articles/1087319/harvard-cyberlaw-clinic-backs-airbnb-in-rowdy-guest-suit) could undermine Communications Decency Act protections of internet startups.
>
