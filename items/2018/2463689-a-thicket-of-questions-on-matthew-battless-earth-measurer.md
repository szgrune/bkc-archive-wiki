> A Thicket of Questions: On Matthew Battles’s Earth Measurer
> 
> [Matthew Battles](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/mbattles), the associate director at [metaLAB](https://metalabharvard.github.io/), explains his project Earth Measurer through a [conversation](https://medium.com/@metalabharvard/a-thicket-of-questions-on-matthew-battless-earth-measurer-6cefa0351add) with Randy Rosenthal, a Communications Consultant at metaLAB. “So I guess the question looks something like this: how does nonliving artificial-intelligence technology relate to the precarious state of life on earth? What happens when we put them into dialogue with one another?” Battles asks in [the Medium post](https://medium.com/@metalabharvard/a-thicket-of-questions-on-matthew-battless-earth-measurer-6cefa0351add).
