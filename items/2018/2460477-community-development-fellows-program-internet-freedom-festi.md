# Community Development Fellows Program | Internet Freedom Festival

**Source:** [internetfreedomfestival.org](https://internetfreedomfestival.org/fellowship/) · **Published:** 2018-08-21 · **Feed tags:** orbit

> Internet Freedom Festival established community development fellowship
> 
> The Internet Freedom Festival (IFF) established its first [Community Development Fellowship](https://internetfreedomfestival.org/fellowship/), through which fellows will identify, connect, and grow local communities working to defend a free and open Internet. Fellows will also play a key role in the Internet Freedom Festival, taking place in Valencia, Spain in April 2019. The fellowship [application](https://internetfreedomfestival.org/community-development-fellowship-application/) is open through August 27.
