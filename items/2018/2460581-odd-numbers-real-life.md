# Odd Numbers — Real Life

**Source:** [reallifemag.com](http://reallifemag.com/odd-numbers/) · **Published:** 2018-08-22 · **Feed tags:** orbit

> Algorithms alone can’t meaningfully hold other algorithms accountable
> 
> “We should not confuse the necessary work to incrementally reform a system with the moral imperative to also question its basic presuppositions,” writes Frank Pasquale in [this post](http://reallifemag.com/odd-numbers/) for Real Life Magazine. Pasquale poses questions such as, is the “imagination” of algorithm use limited by the urge to attract investors? To whom should algorithmic processes be accountable? Read his take on these questions in [Real Life Magazine](http://reallifemag.com/odd-numbers/).
