# Why your online data isn’t safe – Harvard Gazette

**Source:** [news.harvard.edu](https://news.harvard.edu/gazette/story/2018/10/why-your-online-data-isnt-safe/) · **Published:** 2018-10-03 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Why your online data isn’t safe
> 
> Berkman Klein Executive Director [Urs Gasser](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/ugasser)was interviewed by [the Harvard Gazette](https://news.harvard.edu/gazette/story/2018/10/why-your-online-data-isnt-safe/) about recent data breaches and his perspective on current and potential regulations. In the interview, Gasser also shares how users can keep their data safe, and why there isn’t an easy solution to the problem. “Simply put, to really prioritize user privacy would also mean to compromise an underlying business model that has been very successful in economic terms and produced some of the wealthiest companies in the world,” Gasser says.
