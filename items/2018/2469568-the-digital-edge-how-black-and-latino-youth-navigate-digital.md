# The Digital Edge | How Black and Latino Youth Navigate Digital Inequality

**Source:** [nyupress.org](https://nyupress.org/books/9781479849857/) · **Published:** 2018-11-16 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> [](https://nyupress.org/books/9781479849857/)
> Andres Lombana-Bermudez contributes to a new book about how black and Latino youth learn, create, and collaborate online.
> The Digital Edge examines how the digital and social-media lives of low-income youth, especially youth of color, have evolved amidst rapid social and technological change.
