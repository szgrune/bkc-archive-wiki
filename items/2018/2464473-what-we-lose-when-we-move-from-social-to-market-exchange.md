# What we lose when we move from social to market exchange

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/hci-design-at-uw/what-we-lose-when-we-move-from-social-to-market-exchange-e1f80164b50) · **Published:** 2018-10-10 · **Feed tags:** website, mailchimp, buzz, orbit

> What we lose when we move from social to market exchange
> 
> Berkman Klein faculty associate [Benjamin Mako Hill](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/mako) published [a paper](https://medium.com/hci-design-at-uw/what-we-lose-when-we-move-from-social-to-market-exchange-e1f80164b50) on the Couchsurfing and Airbnb websites, trends in their use, what is expected of each service, arguing that their work shows a shift away from social exchange and toward markets, and what this means for the sharing economy. The paper will be presented at the ACM Conference on Computer-supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing.
