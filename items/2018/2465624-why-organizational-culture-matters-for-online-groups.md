> Why organizational culture matters for online groups
> 
> World of Warcraft gave Berkman Klein faculty associate [Benjamin Mako Hill](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/mako) and his coauthors a unique platform to study online communities, which they describe in [this Medium post](https://medium.com/hci-design-at-uw/why-organizational-culture-matters-for-online-groups-347cad5a7c06). The trio found evidence of organizational culture in their research, and call for more work into this culture in online settings. The work will be published in Proceedings of the ACM Conference on Computer-supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing.
