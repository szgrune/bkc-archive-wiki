# Helping Global Policymakers Navigate AI’s Challenges and Opportunities

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/@BKCHarvard/helping-global-policymakers-navigate-ais-challenges-and-opportunities-11b128687cad) · **Published:** 2018-08-13 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Policymakers around the world increasingly find themselves at the center of discussions around AI technologies. "Many regulators did not want or need a grand governance framework for AI," says [Ryan Budish](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/rbudish) in [this piece  summarizing](https://medium.com/berkman-klein-center/helping-global-policymakers-navigate-ais-challenges-and-opportunities-11b128687cad) some of his recent conversations with policymakers. "They wanted practical steps to begin to close some of the many gaps they see and that worry them."
