# The Black Mirror Writers’ Room: Teaching Technology Ethics through Speculation

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/@cfiesler/the-black-mirror-writers-room-teaching-technology-ethics-through-speculation-f1a9e2deccf4) · **Published:** 2018-10-15 · **Feed tags:** website, mailchimp, buzz

> [The Black Mirror Writer's Room: Teaching Technology Ethics through Speculation](https://medium.com/@cfiesler/the-black-mirror-writers-room-teaching-technology-ethics-through-speculation-f1a9e2deccf4)
> 
> Speculation around ethical regulation of futuristic technologies lends itself to compelling discussions about rights and the role of government in a technologically evolving society. Berkman Klein Faculty Associate [Casey Fiesler](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/casey-fiesler) engages her student's in this approach: "There are a lot of important things we can be doing in the world to help, but I’d like to think that teaching technologists to think critically about responsibility and ethics is one way to keep our lives from into turning into a Black Mirror episode."
