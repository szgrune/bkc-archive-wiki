# The Techtopia program adds it's inaugural cohort of students

**Source:** [techtopia.harvard.edu](http://techtopia.harvard.edu/program-overview/) · **Published:** 2018-09-28 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Our Techtopia program is excited to welcome a vibrant community of faculty, fellows, and staff from around Harvard University.
> 
> The program's mission is "to illuminate and expand Harvard’s teaching and research efforts on the ethics and governance of emerging digital technologies, and to equip students with the interdisciplinary problem solving techniques and the unique communication skills required to tackle the pressing social, political, ethical, and legal issues at this intersection."
