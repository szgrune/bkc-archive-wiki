> Match Made in Heaven: Authoritarian States and Digital Surveillance; A Case Study From Azerbaijan
> 
> Former Berkman Klein fellow [Arzu Geybullayeva](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/ageybulla) published her research on the state of information controls in Azerbaijan, which she conducted during her time as a fellow. “Internet might be free and open in Azerbaijan, but so is the ruling power to control, intimidate and eventually, shut down the Internet if such need ever arise,” Geybullayeva writes in [the report.](https://www.opentech.fund/news/match-made-heaven-authoritarian-states-and-digital-surveillance-case-study-azerbaijan/)
