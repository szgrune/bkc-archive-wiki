# AI Initiative Incubates The Markup, a New Investigative Journalism Venture Focused on Revealing the Societal Impact of Technology — AI Initiative

**Source:** [aiethicsinitiative.org](https://aiethicsinitiative.org/news/2018/9/23/ai-initiative-incubates-the-markup-a-new-investigative-journalism-venture-focused-on-revealing-the-societal-impact-of-technology) · **Published:** 2018-09-27 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> AI Initiative Incubates The Markup, a New Investigative Journalism Venture Focused on Revealing the Societal Impact of Technology
> 
> [The Markup](https://themarkup.org/), a nonprofit journalism outlet for providing independent analysis of how technology is changing the news ecosystem, officially launched last week. The Ethics and Governance of AI Initiative, a joint project between the Berkman Klein Center and the MIT Media Lab, was [the first funder](https://aiethicsinitiative.org/news/2018/9/23/ai-initiative-incubates-the-markup-a-new-investigative-journalism-venture-focused-on-revealing-the-societal-impact-of-technology) of The Markup, providing an initial seed grant of $750,000 that supported the founding team as they developed the project over this year.
