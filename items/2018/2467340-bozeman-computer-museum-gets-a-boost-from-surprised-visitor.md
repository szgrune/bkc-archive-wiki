# Bozeman Computer Museum gets a boost from surprised visitor

**Source:** [kbzk.com](https://kbzk.com/news/2018/11/14/bozeman-computer-museum-gets-a-boost-from-surprised-visitor/) · **Published:** 2018-11-14 · **Feed tags:** mailchimp, website, buzz

> [The Museum of Computers and Robotics](http://www.compustory.com/), near the MSU campus in Bozeman, is the world’s oldest museum devoted strictly too computers. It’s also one of the few computer-only museums in the world.
> 
> Open 6 days a week with free admission, the museum, run by robotics expert, George Keremedjiev, recently got a boost from a well-known Harvard software engineer.
> 
> Andromeda Yelton surprised the Museum's community by posting a [twitter thread](https://twitter.com/thatandromeda/status/1027988913904742400?lang=en) that ended up going viral.
> 
> “I got there and was just blown away,” said Yelton who says she was pleasantly surprised after she arrived at the hard-to-find location, “It doesn’t really look like a museum from the outside.”
