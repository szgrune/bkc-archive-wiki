# Toward a safe and open Internet in Pakistan

**Source:** [medium.com](https://medium.com/berkman-klein-center/toward-a-safe-and-open-internet-in-pakistan-37528e8d0506) · **Published:** 2018-08-07 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Toward a safe and open Internet in Pakistan
> 
> Berkman Klein intern [Mary Meisenzahl](http://marymeisenzahl.com/) interviews [Nighat Dad](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/ndad), Executive Director of the[Digital Rights Foundation](https://digitalrightsfoundation.pk/) and affiliate at Berkman Klein, about the Foundation’s ongoing work to help women and vulnerable populations in Pakistan navigate and understand digital rights. “My mission was that the demand for good legislation, or demand for the government to do something about this issue should come from the public or Internet users, not digital rights organizations,” Dad says in this [Medium](https://medium.com/berkman-klein-center/toward-a-safe-and-open-internet-in-pakistan-37528e8d0506) post.
