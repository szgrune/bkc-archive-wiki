# Opinion | Internet Hacking Is About to Get Much Worse - The New York Times

**Source:** [nytimes.com](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/11/opinion/internet-hacking-cybersecurity-iot.html) · **Published:** 2018-10-11 · **Feed tags:** website, mailchimp, buzz

> [Internet Hacking Is About to Get Much Worse](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/11/opinion/internet-hacking-cybersecurity-iot.html)
> 
> In his [New York Times Op-Ed](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/11/opinion/internet-hacking-cybersecurity-iot.html), Berkman Klein Fellow [Bruce Schneier](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/bschneier) discusses computer insecurity and the direct dangers posed to physical spaces. Schneier argues that if our devices, information, and institutions are to remain secure, smart government regulation is inevitable. "We must engage with the future of internet security as a policy issue. Doing so requires a multifaceted approach, one that requires government involvement at every step."
