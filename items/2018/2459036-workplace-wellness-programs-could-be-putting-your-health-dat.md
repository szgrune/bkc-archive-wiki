# Workplace Wellness Programs Could Be Putting Your Health Data at Risk

**Source:** [hbr.org](https://hbr.org/2017/01/workplace-wellness-programs-could-be-putting-your-health-data-at-risk) · **Published:** 2018-08-08 · **Feed tags:** buzz

> Workplace Wellness Programs Could Be Putting Your Health Data at Risk
> 
> Berkman Klein faculty associate [Ifeoma Ajunwa](http://cyber.harvard.edu/people/iajunwa) describes potential privacy implications of workplace wellness programs in [this article](https://hbr.org/2017/01/workplace-wellness-programs-could-be-putting-your-health-data-at-risk)for Harvard Business Review. Ajunwa lists third party services selling data, employer access to an employee’s data, and data breaches as potential threats to privacy. “Employers who want to offer wellness programs should understand the potential legal liabilities, particularly those tied to the collection of big data, and take steps to minimize them,” Ajunwa says.
