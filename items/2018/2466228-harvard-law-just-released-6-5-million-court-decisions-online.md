# Harvard Law Just Released 6.5 Million Court Decisions Online

**Source:** [fortune.com](http://fortune.com/2018/10/29/harvard-law-caselaw/) · **Published:** 2018-10-30 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> In a significant milestone for public access to the law, the Library Innovation Lab at Harvard Law School on Monday published more than 40 million pages of U.S. court decisions online.
> 
> The publication, which represents nearly 6.5 million state and federal court cases, is the culmination of [a five-year project](https://www.nytimes.com/2015/10/29/us/harvard-law-library-sacrifices-a-trove-for-the-sake-of-a-free-database.html) that saw Harvard slice the spines off a vast collection of legal reporter books in order to digitize them.
