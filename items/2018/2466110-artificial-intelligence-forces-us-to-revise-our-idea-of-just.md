# Artificial intelligence forces us to revise our idea of ​​justice

**Source:** [retina.elpais.com](https://retina.elpais.com/retina/2018/10/26/tendencias/1540549298_079415.html) · **Published:** 2018-10-29 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> What it would mean to us if the models with which machine learning understands the world turn out to be more accurate or true than our own way of analyzing how the world works? "It is a long debate, although we are already resorting to machines that think differently from us because they calculate faster or because their answers tend to be more accurate, although we can not explain how they achieve it," says [David Weinberger](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/dweinberger), in [this interview with El Pais](https://retina.elpais.com/retina/2018/10/26/tendencias/1540549298_079415.html).
