# North Bay Village Fl. attorney suing local blogger for libel | Miami Herald

**Source:** [miamiherald.com](https://www.miamiherald.com/news/politics-government/article218333585.html) · **Published:** 2018-09-18 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> City attorney sues blogger for libel. Is it ‘a threat designed to silence?’
> 
> [Kendra Albert](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/kalbert), a Berkman Klein Fellow and instructor at the Cyberlaw Clinic, lends her expertise into [the discussion](https://www.miamiherald.com/news/politics-government/article218333585.html)about a Florida lawsuit between a local blogger and local attorney. “The standard for a public official is not just that what the person said was untrue, but that they had to have known it was untrue — or recklessly disregarded whether it was true or false — and published it anyway. That’s a pretty high bar,” Albert says in the article.
