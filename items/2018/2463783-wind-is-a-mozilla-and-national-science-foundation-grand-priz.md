# Wind is a Mozilla & National Science Foundation Grand Prize Winner – Guardian Project

**Source:** [guardianproject.info](https://guardianproject.info/2018/09/26/wind-is-a-mozilla-national-science-foundation-grand-prize-winner/) · **Published:** 2018-10-02 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Wind is a Mozilla & National Science Foundation Grand Prize Winner
> 
> [Wind](https://guardianproject.info/wind/), a service that provides off-grid communications services to everyday people, [received the grand prize](https://guardianproject.info/2018/09/26/wind-is-a-mozilla-national-science-foundation-grand-prize-winner/) at the Wireless Innovation for a Networked Society (WINS) Challenge from Mozilla and the National Science Foundation. Wind is part of the Guardian Project, which was founded by [Nathanial Freitas](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/nfreitas), a Berkman Klein affiliate.
