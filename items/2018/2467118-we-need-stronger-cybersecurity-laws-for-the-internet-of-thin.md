# We need stronger cybersecurity laws for the Internet of Things

**Source:** [cnn.com](https://www.cnn.com/2018/11/09/opinions/cybersecurity-laws-internet-of-things-schneier/index.html) · **Published:** 2018-11-12 · **Feed tags:** mailchimp, website, buzz

> "...[t]here is no monetary incentive for companies to invest in the cybersecurity measures needed to keep their products secure," writes [Bruce Schneier](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/bschneier) in [this CNN Op-Ed](https://www.cnn.com/2018/11/09/opinions/cybersecurity-laws-internet-of-things-schneier/index.html). "Consumers will buy products without proper security features, unaware that their information is vulnerable. And current liability laws make it hard to hold companies accountable for shoddy software security. It falls upon lawmakers to create laws that protect consumers."
