> Inside the Harvard research hub chronicling our relationship with the internet
> 
> SiliconRepublic talks with Senior Researcher [David O’Brien](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/dobrien) about the multidisciplinary work conducted by the Berkman Klein Center, including the current privacy and cybersecurity projects.
