**Source:** [cyber.harvard.edu](http://cyber.harvard.edu/publication/2018/censorship-and-collateral-damage) · **Published:** 2018-09-05 · **Feed tags:** buzz

> Censorship and Collateral Damage
> 
> Berkman Klein Fellow [Simin Kargar](http://cyber.harvard.edu/people/skargar) and Keith McManamen publish a [new research bulletin](https://cyber.harvard.edu/publication/2018/censorship-and-collateral-damage) about Iran’s ban of the popular messaging application, Telegram. “Even resorting to the ambitious and unrealistic strategy of blocking international Internet infrastructure has not substantially scaled the user base of domestic alternatives to Telegram thus far,” Kagar and McNanamen write.
