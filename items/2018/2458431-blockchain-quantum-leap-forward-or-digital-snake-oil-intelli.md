# Blockchain: Quantum leap forward or digital snake oil? : Intelligence Squared

**Source:** [intelligencesquared.com](https://www.intelligencesquared.com/events/blockchain-quantum-leap-forward-or-digital-snake-oil/) · **Published:** 2018-08-03 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> “[Blockchain] has revolutionary potential, in terms of decentralization, in terms of disintermediation, transparency, accountability. So it can actually create new structures or modify the internal structure of existing institutions, which I think can have a very important impact,” says Faculty Associate [Primavera De Filippi](http://cyber.harvard.edu/people/pdefilippi), on an [Intelligence Squared](https://www.intelligencesquared.com/events/blockchain-quantum-leap-forward-or-digital-snake-oil/) panel discussing Blockchain, what it is, and whether it is deserving of its current hype.
