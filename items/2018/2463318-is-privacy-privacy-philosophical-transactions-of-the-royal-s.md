# Is privacy privacy | Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society of London A: Mathematical, Physical and Engineering Sciences

**Source:** [rsta.royalsocietypublishing.org](http://rsta.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/376/2128/20170358) · **Published:** 2018-09-25 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> Is privacy privacy?
> 
> Berkman Klein Fellow [Alexandra Wood](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/awood)and [Kobbi Nissim](http://people.cs.georgetown.edu/~kobbi/), a Professor at Georgetown University, publish [a paper](http://rsta.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/376/2128/20170358) on the differing and evolving notions of privacy across normative and technical perspectives. “Adopting an understanding of privacy that is consistent across its technical and normative dimensions will be critical to ensuring personal data are adequately safeguarded over the long term,” the authors write, noting that this is a difficult feat to achieve.
