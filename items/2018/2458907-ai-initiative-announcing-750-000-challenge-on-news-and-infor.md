# AI Initiative Announcing $750,000 Challenge on News and Information Quality — AI Initiative

**Source:** [aiethicsinitiative.org](https://aiethicsinitiative.org/news/2018/8/5/ai-initiative-announcing-750000-challenge-on-news-and-information-quality) · **Published:** 2018-09-06 · **Feed tags:** buzz

> AI Initiative Announcing $750,000 Challenge on News and Information Quality
> 
> The [Ethics and Governance of AI Initiative](https://aiethicsinitiative.org/), a joint collaboration between the Berkman Klein Center and the MIT Media Lab, [announced](https://aiethicsinitiative.org/news/2018/8/5/ai-initiative-announcing-750000-challenge-on-news-and-information-quality) a $750,000 Challenge on News and Information Quality. The challenge revolves around the question,“How might we ensure that the use of artificial intelligence in the news ecosystem be done ethically and in the public interest?” Topics of interest include governance, platform design, bad actors, and journalism.
