> Warning Kids About Digital Privacy Doesn't Work. Here's What Does.
> 
> [Sandra Cortesi](http://cyber.harvard.edu/people/scortesi), director of the [Youth and Media](http://cyber.harvard.edu/research/youthandmedia) project and fellow at BKC, is quoted in this [Consumer Reports article](https://www.consumerreports.org/privacy/kids-and-digital-privacy-what-works/) on tips for talking to children about digital privacy. “Contrary to many myths, kids care very much about privacy,” Cortesi says.
