# How Turkey silences journalists online, one removal request at a time - Committee to Protect Journalists

**Source:** [cpj.org](https://cpj.org/blog/2018/08/how-turkey-silences-journalists-online-one-removal.php) · **Published:** 2018-08-20 · **Feed tags:** website, buzz

> How Turkey silences journalists online, one removal request at a time
> 
> The Committee to Protect Journalists (CPJ) used Berkman Klein’s [Lumen database](http://cyber.harvard.edu/research/lumen), in conjunction with other sources, to triangulate censored Twitter accounts of media outlets and journalists in Turkey, for a recent [CPJ blog](https://cpj.org/blog/2018/08/how-turkey-silences-journalists-online-one-removal.php) post. The blog delinates the disproportionate censorship of journalists in Turkey by way of Twitter's "country withheld content" (CWC) policy.
