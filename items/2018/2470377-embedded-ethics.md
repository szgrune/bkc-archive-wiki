# Embedded EthiCS

**Source:** [embeddedethics.seas.harvard.edu](http://embeddedethics.seas.harvard.edu/) · **Published:** 2018-12-17 · **Feed tags:** buzz, website

> Embedded EthiCS is a collaboration between philosophers and computer scientists led by faculty from both fields. Post-doctoral fellows and advanced graduate students meet weekly for a teaching lab to develop Embedded EthiCS modules.
> 
> Embedded EthiCS meets the challenge of making ethical reasoning integral to computer science education with a distributed pedagogy that introduces ethics directly into standard computer science courses across the curriculum. It works by embedding philosophers into courses to teach a module for the course that explores an ethical issue that the course material raises.
