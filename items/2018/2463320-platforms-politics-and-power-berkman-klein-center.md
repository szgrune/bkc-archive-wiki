# Platforms, Politics, and Power | Berkman Klein Center

**Source:** [cyber.harvard.edu](https://cyber.harvard.edu/events/2018-09-18/platforms-politics-and-power) · **Published:** 2018-09-25 · **Feed tags:** buzz

> Platforms, Politics, and Power: Understanding and Shaping the Internet in 2018
> 
> Berkman Klein Faculty Chair and Director [Jonathan Zittrain](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/jzittrain) spoke about “[Platforms, Politics, and Power](https://cyber.harvard.edu/events/2018-09-18/platforms-politics-and-power)” during the Berkman Klein Luncheon Series. Zittrain overview of the internet since its inception, spanning the debates, concerns, and hopes in the years since, and how the Berkman Klein Center fits into—and contributes to—these conversations. “We have, as part of our credo, want to build on the Internet as much as study it and write papers about it, or talk to policymakers about it,” Zittrain said.
