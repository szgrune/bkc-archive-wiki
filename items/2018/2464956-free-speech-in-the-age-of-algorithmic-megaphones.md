# Free Speech in the Age of Algorithmic Megaphones

**Source:** [wired.com](https://www.wired.com/story/facebook-domestic-disinformation-algorithmic-megaphones/) · **Published:** 2018-10-15 · **Feed tags:** website, mailchimp, buzz

> [Algorithmic Megaphones and Free Speech](https://www.wired.com/story/facebook-domestic-disinformation-algorithmic-megaphones/)
> 
> Where is the line between authentic free speech and the manipulation of speech? Is the right to speech equated with the right to reach millions of people? [Renee Diresta](https://cyber.harvard.edu/people/rdiresta), Berkman Klein Affiliate, discusses these questions [in her recent article](https://www.wired.com/story/facebook-domestic-disinformation-algorithmic-megaphones/) about Facebook's account takedowns and the robust complaints around cencorship that Facebook gamers and fake account creators adopt to fight back.
