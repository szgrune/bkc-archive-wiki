# [Hommage] Elle s’appelait Marielle Franco… – Jeune Afrique

**Source:** [jeuneafrique.com](https://www.jeuneafrique.com/mag/910286/societe/hommage-elle-sappelait-marielle-franco/) · **Published:** 2020-04-03 · **Feed tags:** addedcommunity, community

> Marielle Franco represented the future of politics. Not only was she a symbol for the renewal of political leadership, elected councilwoman that was at the intersection of many minority identities- A Black Lesbian,  born in the favelas and   had a child when she was a teen. She also advocated for all these communities that she identified for and worked for their better inclusion in a more egalitarian society.
