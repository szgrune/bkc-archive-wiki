# COVID-19: A spotlight on child data governance gaps | UNICEF Office of Global Insight & Policy

**Source:** [unicef.org](https://www.unicef.org/globalinsight/reports/covid-19-spotlight-child-data-governance-gaps) · **Published:** 2020-09-03 · **Feed tags:** addedcommunity, community

> Some of the challenges related to the protection of children’s data during the COVID-19 pandemic are not new and will continue into the foreseeable future, unless we collectively articulate better that the world we want for children is one where their rights are respected, data about them is safeguarded and is not
> exploited for commercial and other purposes.
