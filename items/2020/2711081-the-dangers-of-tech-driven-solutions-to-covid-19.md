# The dangers of tech-driven solutions to COVID-19

**Source:** [brookings.edu](https://www.brookings.edu/techstream/the-dangers-of-tech-driven-solutions-to-covid-19/) · **Published:** 2020-06-17 · **Feed tags:** addedcommunity, community

> "[H]abits of deferring to private-sector “solutions” to collective problems have undermined our capacity for effective pandemic response. What’s more, failures to hold tech firms accountable for their uses of personal information have actually made us more vulnerable to prolonged, uncontainable outbreaks."
