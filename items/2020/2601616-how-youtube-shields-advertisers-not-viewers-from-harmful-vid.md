# How YouTube shields advertisers (not viewers) from harmful videos — Quartz

**Source:** [qz.com](https://qz.com/1785613/how-youtube-shields-advertisers-not-viewers-from-harmful-videos/) · **Published:** 2020-01-22 · **Feed tags:** addedcommunity, community

> Jonas Kaiser, researcher at the Berkman Klein Center for Internet & Society at Harvard University, notes that YouTube faces questions of censorship and freedom of speech when it comes to what videos are permitted on the platform. “The relationship YouTube has with advertisers is more straightforward,” he says, adding that YouTube protects itself from suffering financially by working to remove ads from harmful content.
