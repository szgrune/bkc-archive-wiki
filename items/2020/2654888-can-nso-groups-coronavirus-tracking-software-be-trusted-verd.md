# Can NSO Group’s coronavirus tracking software be trusted? | Verdict

**Source:** [verdict.co.uk](https://www.verdict.co.uk/nso-group-coronavirus-software/) · **Published:** 2020-04-06 · **Feed tags:** addedcommunity, community

> In a blog post discussing the use of tracking tools during the pandemic, Elizabeth M Renieris, a Fellow at Harvard’s Berkman Klein Center for Internet & Society, questioned whether such tools would actually make a difference. “What if it just doesn’t work in some contexts? We especially have to ask these questions where some experimental methods of contact tracing are being entrusted to large for-profit tech companies,” she wrote.
