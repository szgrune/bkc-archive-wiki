# Surveillance & Immigration Tech

One of the most concentrated and alarmed threads of 2025: the build-out of a US
**domestic surveillance apparatus**, with **ICE** as the recurring protagonist —
facial recognition (Clearview AI), bulk phone-location tracking, a central database
fusing health/labor/housing data, and a "social media panopticon." Adjacent items
cover commercial **spyware** (Paragon), the far-right politics behind the underlying
tech, and DOGE's construction of a surveillance state. 404 Media and Mother Jones do
much of the investigative reporting; the **Harvard Cyberlaw Clinic** appears directly,
representing advocates and researchers in social-media-surveillance litigation. The
through-line: surveillance infrastructure built for one purpose is rapidly
repurposed against immigrants and dissenters.
