# AI Safety, Risk & Agents

Where the archive engages the frontier-risk and technical-governance conversation —
though notably through a **skeptical, institution-focused** lens rather than a
doomer one. The signature 2025 development is the rise of **agentic AI** and the
question of how to govern autonomous agents (regulating their "agency," the "OODA
loop problem," autonomous AI hacking). The International AI Safety Report tracks
capabilities and risk; arXiv preprints supply frontier-risk methods. Running
alongside is a strand of **satire and critique** of the alignment industry itself
(the recurring alignmentalignment.ai items, also tagged `bkc-happenings`), and debate
over whether frontier AI "should be open-source."
