# AI Governance & Regulation

The archive's single largest 2025 thread. The dominant US story is a fight over
**who gets to regulate AI** — a proposed federal moratorium on state-level rules
versus California's **SB 53** transparency law — set against a Trump administration
pushing deregulation and a leaked AI executive order framed by critics as a "Big Tech
power grab." A parallel international story tracks **EU divergence**: the Digital
Omnibus rollback and EU-vs-US comparisons of how to discipline Big Tech. A recurring
analytic move across these sources is to reject the "regulation vs. innovation"
framing as a false binary and to read deregulation as itself a policy choice that
reallocates corporate power.
