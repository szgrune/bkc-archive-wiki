# AI, Copyright & Knowledge Institutions

How AI is reshaping the institutions that make and keep knowledge — **copyright,
journalism, and libraries**. The 2025 legal spine is the **Anthropic settlement** and
a SCOTUS amicus brief on copyright & AI, alongside research extracting memorized
copyrighted books from open-weight models. A second strand defends **knowledge
institutions** as more important in a "post-authenticity AI age": the "war on
libraries," AI-and-historical-newspapers, and Harvard's Library Innovation Lab work on
datasets. A third tracks **journalism's** struggle — "journalism minus the journalist,"
newsrooms vs. AI scraping, and dedicated AI-journalism fellowships. Connects to the
epistemics worries in [[ai-chatbots-and-mental-health]].
