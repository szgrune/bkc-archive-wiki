# Content Moderation & Online Speech

The archive's long-running platform-governance beat, anchored in 2025 by two events:
the **US Supreme Court's TikTok ruling** and **Meta's retreat from fact-checking**.
Recurring sub-threads: First Amendment doctrine and "chilling effects" (heavily via
the Knight First Amendment Institute), the migration to open networks like **Bluesky**
and its trust-and-safety growing pains, and the use of **LLMs for moderation** itself.
A darker 2025 turn is the weaponization of moderation and visa policy against
speech — Trump's visa restrictions targeting content-moderation workers, and AI used
to hunt activists. [[evelyn-douek]] is the recurring scholarly voice on what's
happening to the First Amendment online.
