# AI & the Environment

A smaller but sharply-defined 2025 thread on the **physical footprint** of the AI
boom: the energy and water demands of data centers, and the local communities
absorbing the cost. The framing tightens over the year — from "how much power does AI
consume?" to concrete siting conflicts ("the precedent is Flint" on Oregon's water
crisis) and the politics of building data centers on federal land. Resource-extraction
framings (lithium/copper mining, "the cloud is dead") connect this topic to
[[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]] and to the extraction thesis in
[[ai-labor-and-economy]].
