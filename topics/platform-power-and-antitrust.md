# Platform Power & Antitrust

The structural-power lens: market concentration, antitrust enforcement, and control of
critical digital infrastructure. 2025's pivot point is **Meta winning its monopoly
trial** (and what that "reveals about cracks in antitrust enforcement"), alongside the
**US v. Google** generative-AI remedy debate and Europe's first **DMA** fines against
Apple and Meta. A deeper structural strand targets the **cloud oligopoly** — scrutiny
of Google/Microsoft/Amazon investments, "engineering the cloud commons," and
antimonopoly approaches to AI. Tim Wu's *Age of Extraction* is the connective thesis
linking concentration to the labor and value-capture concerns in
[[ai-labor-and-economy]].
