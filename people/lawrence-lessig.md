# Lawrence Lessig

A BKC co-founder, Harvard Law professor, and founder of **Creative Commons** — the
intellectual origin of the archive's [[copyright-and-open-access|copyright & open-access]]
thread. His "free culture" argument (that overbroad copyright chills creativity and that
"code is law") shaped the Center's earliest agenda and recurs throughout the Buzz years.
The archive then tracks his later turns: a continued critique of copyright maximalism
(opposing Congress's copyright-term extension) and, most recently, the argument that
**AI and TikTok are producing a free-speech crisis** — connecting the old IP fights to
today's [[content-moderation-and-speech]] and [[ai-governance-and-regulation]] debates.
