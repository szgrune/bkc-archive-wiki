# danah boyd

Founder and president of [[data-society|Data & Society]], partner researcher at
Microsoft Research, and a longtime BKC affiliate. She is the archive's defining voice on
**youth, social media, and privacy** — her 2014 book *It's Complicated* and the
"Privacy at the Margins" agenda (the basis of the [[2014-privacy-at-the-margins|first
event page]]) reframe privacy as unevenly distributed and structurally shaped rather
than a uniform individual right. The Buzz years feature her steadily ("corrupt
personalization," teens and "selling out," new survey findings on teen privacy), and
through Data & Society she remains central to [[misinformation-and-elections]] and
[[privacy-and-surveillance]].
