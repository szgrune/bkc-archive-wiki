# Evelyn Douek

Legal scholar of online speech and platform governance, and the archive's recurring
authority on **what is happening to the First Amendment online**. In 2025 she is a
named author on the critique of the Supreme Court's TikTok ruling (with Jameel Jaffer)
and the subject of explainer interviews on social-media speech doctrine. A reliable
anchor for the [[content-moderation-and-speech]] thread.
