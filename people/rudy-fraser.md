# Rudy Fraser

Builder of **Blacksky**, a community-run service and moderation layer on the AT
Protocol / Bluesky designed by and for Black users. The most-recurring individual in
the 2025 slice — appearing across podcasts, talks, and panels — which makes him a
useful case study for the archive's interest in **decentralized, community-controlled
infrastructure** as an alternative to centralized platform governance. His framing
("No one's coming to save us — communities must build their own internet") ties the
decentralization thread to questions of equity and self-determination.
