# John Palfrey

A BKC co-founder and its former executive director, later Head of School at Phillips
Academy Andover and, since 2019, president of the **MacArthur Foundation**. In the Buzz
years he is the archive's leading voice on **libraries and access to knowledge** — the
recurring champion of the **Digital Public Library of America (DPLA)**, whose launch and
anniversaries he narrates — as well as on youth and digital life (*Born Digital*) and
internet governance. His move to MacArthur (a major tech-policy funder) is itself a
node connecting BKC's alumni network to the field's philanthropy. Foundational to
[[ai-copyright-and-knowledge|knowledge institutions]] and [[berkman-klein-center]].
