# David Weinberger

A longtime BKC fellow, technologist and philosopher of the web (co-author of *The
Cluetrain Manifesto*; author of *Too Big to Know* and *Everyday Chaos*). One of the most
frequently featured voices of the Buzz era — his "From David" columns recur for years —
and a bridge from the early-web optimism to the machine-learning present. His
preoccupation is **epistemology**: how networks, and now AI, change the shape of
knowledge, order, and prediction. The later items track that turn directly (machine
learning and "confidence everywhere," *Everyday Chaos*, "keeping marketing human"),
making him a useful entry point to [[ai-safety-and-agents]] and
[[ai-copyright-and-knowledge]].
