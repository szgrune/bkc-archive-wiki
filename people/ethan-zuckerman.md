# Ethan Zuckerman

A foundational BKC figure: co-founder of [[global-voices]] (2005) and longtime Center
affiliate, now director of the Initiative for Digital Public Infrastructure at UMass
Amherst. He is one of the few names that recurs across the **entire** span of the
archive — from the Buzz years through the present — and his throughline is remarkably
consistent: a search for **civic, public-interest alternatives to commercial social
media**. The later items map that project directly ("Can Public Infrastructure Fix
Social Media?", "The Good Web," "Beyond Facebook Logic," a social-network taxonomy),
while his journalism work anchors [[journalism-and-the-news-business]]. A natural hub
page linking [[platform-power-and-antitrust]] and [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]].
