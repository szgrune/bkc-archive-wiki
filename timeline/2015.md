# 2015

**The Buzz signs off.** Only a half-year of newsletters survives (the Berkman Buzz ends
in May 2015), but the issues capture the debates that carry into the platform decade.

## The year's threads
- **The "Right to be Forgotten."** Repeated debate over Europe's erasure right and
  internet anonymity — [[privacy-and-surveillance]] and
  [[content-moderation-and-speech]].
- **Zero rating and access.** A pointed critique of **Internet.org**'s "failure" and
  zero-rating, plus mapping the net-neutrality debate — [[net-neutrality-and-internet-access]]
  and [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]].
- **Security, again.** "Cybersecurity and the Torah," "security theater," tracing the
  Sony hack — [[cybersecurity-and-encryption]].
- **A web at 25.** Tim Berners-Lee and "a new manifesto for the Web"; women in tech at
  Berkman; youth and digital media.
