# 2011

The year of **networked protest and the copyright wars**. The Buzz tracks the Arab
Spring and Occupy in real time while the **SOPA/PIPA** fight mobilizes the open-internet
movement at home.

## The year's threads
- **Revolutions, online.** The Arab Spring (Tunisia, **#Amina**, Egypt) and **Occupy /
  #OWS** run through the year — [[content-moderation-and-speech|networked speech]] and
  [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south|global internet freedom]], with "internet kill
  switches" and the Jasmine Revolution's filtering.
- **The copyright uprising.** **SOPA/PIPA** and ACTA galvanize
  [[copyright-and-open-access|copyright and open-access]] activism (the end of Righthaven,
  too).
- **Libraries and knowledge.** The **Digital Public Library of America** push begins (see
  [[john-palfrey]]); **Media Cloud** and **Herdict** mature as research tools.
- **End of an era.** Steve Jobs's death prompts reflection on the PC's future
  ([[jonathan-zittrain]]'s generativity question).
