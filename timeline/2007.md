# 2007

A full year of weekly Buzz (51 issues), and the year BKC's **global internet-freedom**
infrastructure takes shape. The throughline is building the institutions to *study and
defend* online expression worldwide.

## The year's threads
- **Citizen media goes global.** [[global-voices|Global Voices]] and the new **Citizen
  Media Law Project** anchor the year — legal protection and a platform for bloggers
  worldwide. Roots of [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]].
- **Mapping censorship.** The **OpenNet Initiative**'s research on state internet
  filtering (notably [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south|China]]) becomes a signature
  output; **Tor** circulation grows.
- **Copyright and the early platforms.** [[copyright-and-open-access|Copyright and the
  DMCA]] stay central as **Facebook** first enters the conversation.

## Notable voices
Doc Searls, [[lawrence-lessig]], Wendy Seltzer, [[david-weinberger]], Rebecca MacKinnon,
and Dan Gillmor. See [[berkman-klein-center]].
