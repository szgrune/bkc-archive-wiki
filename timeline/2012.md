# 2012

**Open access ascendant**, in the long shadow of SOPA. The year pairs a maturing
free-knowledge agenda with the first wave of online-education enthusiasm.

## The year's threads
- **The open-access year.** Repeated campaigning for [[copyright-and-open-access|open
  access]] ("the inevitability of open access," the Declaration of Internet Freedom) and
  the SOPA/PIPA debate mapped online.
- **Education goes online.** MOOCs and "a new kind of online course" arrive — early
  [[ai-copyright-and-knowledge|knowledge-institution]] disruption.
- **Global censorship, continued.** Ethiopia, Pakistan's filtering, Russia's censorship
  bill, and **Kony 2012** — [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]] via
  [[global-voices|Global Voices]].
- **Youth & media.** New BKC research on bullying "in a networked era" and digital
  civics.
