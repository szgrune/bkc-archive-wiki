# 2018

**Cambridge Analytica and the techlash in full.** One of the archive's two largest years:
platform accountability becomes the organizing story, with privacy law, disinformation,
and algorithmic fairness all surging at once.

## The year's threads
- **Platform accountability.** Facebook under scrutiny across the year — the spine of
  [[platform-power-and-antitrust]] and [[content-moderation-and-speech]].
- **Privacy gets teeth.** GDPR takes effect and data-protection coverage spikes —
  [[privacy-and-surveillance]].
- **Disinformation as a field.** Russian influence operations and fact-checking
  infrastructure — [[misinformation-and-elections]].
- **Fairness and spyware.** "Algorithm-in-the-loop" fairness research
  ([[algorithmic-accountability]]); Citizen Lab's **Reckless** NSO/Pegasus series
  ([[cybersecurity-and-encryption]]).
