# 2017

The **TagTeam feed comes online** and the **techlash begins**. After 2016, the dominant
question is what the platforms did to democracy — and the curated feed starts capturing
it in earnest.

## The year's threads
- **The 2016 reckoning.** Russian propaganda's reach, "fake news," and the Center/
  Shorenstein report finding conservative media dominated 2016 coverage — see
  [[misinformation-and-elections]] and [[platform-power-and-antitrust]].
- **Algorithms enter the dock.** "How Facebook's oracular algorithm decides," algorithms
  in the justice system at Harvard Law — [[algorithmic-accountability]].
- **Net neutrality's last stand.** The FCC repeal fight, with the Cyberlaw Clinic
  litigating — [[net-neutrality-and-internet-access]].
- **Surveillance, local and global.** A Cambridge surveillance ordinance
  ([[privacy-and-surveillance]]); Citizen Lab's first spyware reports
  ([[cybersecurity-and-encryption]]).
