# 2014

**Net neutrality, encryption, and the platform frictions.** A dense year in which the
post-Snowden security debate, the net-neutrality fight, and early platform-economy
discontents all run at once. It is also where the **TagTeam** feed first overlaps the
Buzz — the archive's only real cross-source window (see [[2014-privacy-at-the-margins]]).

## The year's threads
- **Net neutrality as a rights issue.** "How net neutrality affects human rights" and how
  to advocate for it — see [[net-neutrality-and-internet-access]].
- **Encryption after Snowden.** Apple's iPhone encryption, CSEC revelations, and "the
  case for Snowden immunity" — [[cybersecurity-and-encryption]].
- **Platform discontents.** Uber's "hubris," **Gamergate** data, "corrupt
  personalization," and big-data [[privacy-and-surveillance|privacy]] worries;
  [[danah-boyd]]'s *It's Complicated*.
- **Identity & the body.** India's Aadhaar [[facial-recognition-and-deepfakes|biometrics]]
  program; Bitcoin's regulatory questions; the Sony hack.
