# Lawfare

Law-and-national-security publication (lawfareblog.com, later lawfaremedia.org) that
supplies the archive's **legal and governance** analysis, often in long-form podcast and
essay form. Its presence runs from 2019 — Facebook's Oversight Board, the Chesney &
Citron deepfakes work that named the threat, the "commercial surveillance emergency" —
through 2025–26 comparative Big Tech regulation (EU v. US), "data colonialism" political
economy, digital threat modeling under authoritarianism, and the *Age of Extraction*
conversation with [[tim-wu]].
