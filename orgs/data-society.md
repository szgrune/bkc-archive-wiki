# Data & Society Research Institute

The archive's most-cited **peer research institute** — an independent New York center
(founded 2014 by [[danah-boyd]]) studying the social implications of data and AI. It
appears in two modes: as a **publisher of public-interest research** and as a
**convener** of events and fellowships. Its outputs supply some of the archive's
sharpest counter-narratives to AI-industry framing — "Dispelling Myths of AI and
Efficiency," "Red-Teaming in the Public Interest," "The Cloud is Dead," "Scam GPT" —
and its "Source Hacking" work is a foundational reference in
[[misinformation-and-elections]]. A natural sibling institution to the
[[berkman-klein-center]].
