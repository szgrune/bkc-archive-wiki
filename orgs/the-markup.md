# The Markup

A nonprofit, data-driven **investigative tech-accountability newsroom** (now part of
CalMatters) that supplies the archive with some of its most concrete adversarial
reporting. Where analysis outlets argue, The Markup *measures* — auditing the 650,000
advertising labels assigned to users, documenting Instagram's moderation beyond
shadowbanning, exposing campus surveillance, and tracing how gig-economy laws go
unenforced. Its two-part run on **Section 230** ("a load-bearing wall") is a key
reference for [[content-moderation-and-speech]]. Reporting clusters in
[[platform-power-and-antitrust]], [[privacy-and-surveillance]], and
[[ai-labor-and-economy]].
