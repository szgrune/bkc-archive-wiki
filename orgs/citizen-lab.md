# The Citizen Lab

An interdisciplinary laboratory at the University of Toronto's Munk School (directed by
Ron Deibert) that is the archive's **primary source on mercenary spyware and digital
transnational repression**. Its forensic investigations turned NSO Group's Pegasus into
a named, documented threat: the multi-part **"Reckless"** series tracking spyware
deployed against Mexican journalists and their families, the targeting of a *New York
Times* journalist by a Saudi-linked operator, and Saudi espionage against a Canadian
resident. It also documents state information control directly ("Censored Contagion" on
China's COVID-era social-media censorship). The backbone of
[[cybersecurity-and-encryption]] and a recurring actor in
[[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]].
