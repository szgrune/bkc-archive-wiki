# Global Voices

An international **citizen-media network** co-founded at the Berkman Center in 2005 by
[[ethan-zuckerman]] and Rebecca MacKinnon — one of BKC's most enduring spin-offs. It
recurs across the full span of the archive, from the Buzz years (Global Voices at SXSW,
unpacking "Kony 2012") to the present, and is the archive's earliest and most sustained
window onto **global / citizen journalism and digital rights in the Global South**. Its
**Advox** advocacy arm and country-researcher network surface censorship and speech
stories the mainstream feed misses; its 2024 summit's question — "where have all the
bloggers gone?" — captures the decline of the open-blogging era it grew from. A
foundational actor in [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]].
