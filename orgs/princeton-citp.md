# Princeton Center for Information Technology Policy (CITP)

Princeton's interdisciplinary tech-policy center — a **sibling institution to the
[[berkman-klein-center]]** that recurs in the archive chiefly as a **convener**: its
weekly seminar series, technology-fellows and emerging-scholars programs, and public
talks. The seminar listings double as a map of the academic tech-policy field's current
questions — evidence-based tech policy, online communities, aging online, the economics
of platform accounts, AI and journalism. Its strong presence (one of the most frequent
non-Harvard domains) reflects the BKC feed's role as a clearinghouse for the broader
academic network's events and opportunities.
