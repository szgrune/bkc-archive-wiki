# Berkman Klein Center for Internet & Society (BKC)

The institutional home of this archive — Harvard's center for Internet & society, whose
curated feed (TagTeam hub 1176) *is* the source. Items tagged **`bkc-happenings`** flag
BKC's own news, fellows, and affiliates, and the **Cyberlaw Clinic** (clinic.cyber.harvard.edu)
and **Library Innovation Lab** (lil.law.harvard.edu) appear directly as actors. Reading
the BKC-tagged items together gives a picture of the Center's 2025 priorities:
agentic-AI trust, AI's effect on knowledge institutions, the harms of "dead" AI
systems, and direct litigation against social-media surveillance.
