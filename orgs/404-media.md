# 404 Media

Journalist-owned investigative tech outlet that does much of the archive's **on-the-
ground surveillance reporting**. In 2025 its scoops define the
[[surveillance-and-immigration-tech]] thread: ICE buying bulk phone-location tools,
agents scanning faces on the street, Clearview AI contracts, and a planned central
database fusing health/labor/housing data. Since its 2023 founding it has owned this
beat — ICE's "derogatory speech" database, facial-recognition stalking, the AI-porn
marketplace, AI in hospitals, opt-out tracking audits — and also reports the "war on
libraries" angle in [[ai-copyright-and-knowledge]]. Where Tech Policy Press supplies
analysis, 404 Media supplies the documents. See also
[[facial-recognition-and-deepfakes]].
