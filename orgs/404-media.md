# 404 Media

Journalist-owned investigative tech outlet that does much of the archive's **on-the-
ground surveillance reporting**. In 2025 its scoops define the
[[surveillance-and-immigration-tech]] thread: ICE buying bulk phone-location tools,
agents scanning faces on the street, Clearview AI contracts, and a planned central
database fusing health/labor/housing data. It also reports the "war on libraries"
angle in [[ai-copyright-and-knowledge]]. Where Tech Policy Press supplies analysis, 404
Media supplies the documents.
