# Rest of World

Nonprofit publication covering technology **outside the Western bubble**, and the
archive's primary source for the [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]] thread (52
items across the corpus; see [[_domains|Sources by domain]]). Its reporting supplies the
global-majority perspective that distinguishes this archive from a typical US-centric
tech feed. The earlier years cover gig-worker organizing, El Salvador's bitcoin gamble,
and why "the rest of the world shrugged at the Facebook Papers"; the 2025–26 reporting
turns to AI's global underside — hidden African AI labor, foreign-AI dependence,
AI-fueled mining, energy strain, and how AI shaped elections worldwide.
