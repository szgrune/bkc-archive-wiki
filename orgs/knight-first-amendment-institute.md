# Knight First Amendment Institute

Columbia-based institute (knightcolumbia.org) that is the archive's scholarly engine for
**online speech and First Amendment** questions. Its 2025 output anchors
[[content-moderation-and-speech]]: empirical work on "chilling effects," analysis of
recommender/feed design, the "material support" law's online shadow, and AI agents and
democratic resilience. Closely associated with [[evelyn-douek]].
