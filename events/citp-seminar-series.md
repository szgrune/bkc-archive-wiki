# CITP Seminar Series

The weekly public seminar of [[princeton-citp|Princeton's Center for Information
Technology Policy]] — the single most frequent recurring event in the feed (~24 listings)
and, read together, a rolling index of the academic tech-policy field's live questions:
algorithmic fairness, the measurement of "algorithm skills," digital discrimination law,
the economics of online accounts. The seminar's running presence is why CITP is one of
the archive's most-cited non-Harvard sources.
