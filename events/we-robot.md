# We Robot

The premier **law-and-policy conference on robotics and AI**, recurring in the feed from
2019 to 2025. Long before generative AI, We Robot was the venue where legal scholars
worked out liability, agency, and governance for autonomous systems — making it an early
home for what is now [[ai-safety-and-agents]] and [[ai-governance-and-regulation]].
