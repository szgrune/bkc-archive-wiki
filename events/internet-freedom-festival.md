# Internet Freedom Festival

The annual Valencia gathering (2016–2019 in the feed) of the **global internet-freedom
community** — activists, technologists, and journalists working on censorship
circumvention and digital security in repressive environments. It anchors the
practitioner side of [[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]] and
[[cybersecurity-and-encryption]], the descendant of the Buzz-era OpenNet/Herdict
tradition.
