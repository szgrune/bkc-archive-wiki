# RightsCon

The world's leading summit on **human rights in the digital age**, convened annually by
Access Now and a recurring fixture in the feed from 2017 onward. BKC participates
directly ("Your Guide to BKC@RightsCon 2018"), and the conference doubles as a barometer
of the digital-rights field's anxieties — by 2026 the story is its own contested venue
(the cancelled Zambia edition, China's disruption of the summit). A hub for
[[digital-colonialism-and-global-south]], [[content-moderation-and-speech]], and
[[cybersecurity-and-encryption]].
